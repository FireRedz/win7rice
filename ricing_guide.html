@01 - Windows Programs & Utilities of Interest

Highly recommended you take a look at these programs and utilities as they can save you time and prevent a lot of common fuck ups when it comes to patching or modifying windows. This image reference covers antivirus and a lot of common software: http://www.gbgl-hq.com/demoness/A_g_users_casual_tips.jpg

-Take Control-
(Easiest way to "take ownership" of files, so you can replace your explorerframe, shellstyle, etc.)
http://www.howtogeek.com/howto/windows-vista/add-take-ownership-to-explorer-right-click-menu-in-vista/

-Universal Theme Patcher-
(needed to install third party themes)
http://deepxw.blogspot.com/2008/11/universal-theme-patcher.html

-Theme Installer-
(easier shell/file replacements)
http://www.door2windows.com/windows-theme-installer-install-themes-in-windows-7-vista-with-ease/

-Aves Vista Style Builder-
For theme editing/creation on Win7
http://www.thepiratebay.se/torrent/5923933
Guide: http://virtualcustoms.net/showthread.php/28810-Complete-Guide-to-Theme-Building
(registration required to view links/images)

-TrueTransparency-
An application that allows you to use PNG images to create skins used to change the transparency, and boarder effects of windows. Does not work on everything however, and can be a bit unstable in Win7
http://www.softpedia.com/get/System/OS-Enhancements/TrueTransparency.shtml

(explorerframe, other menu buttons)
